Introduction

Visualizing traffic offenders across Australia

This website introduces a visual analytics project focused on traffic offenders in Australia. It brings together dashboard-based storytelling around three main issue areas: drug-related offences, speeding behaviour, and alcohol-related incidents.

The goal is to make complex datasets easier to read, compare, and present through a cleaner visual structure. You can begin with either of the two dashboards currently available below.
